About us

Who We Are

Duolife pharmaceuticals ltd is a pharmaceutical company that was founded in the year 2018 and incorporated in 2019 as a limited company.
Right from inception the company was focused on pharmaceuticals, non-pharmaceuticals with the aim of creating brand loyalties for their own products or manufacturers brands.

Duolife pharmaceuticals ltd believes in developing a long-term relationship with its suppliers, wholesalers, institutions, retailing chemists, hospitals, nursing homes, NGOs and stocking clinics.
We are also focused on offering quality and affordable medicines to doctors and nurses in their day to day practice.

The company was started as an idea conceived by one of the directors after acquiring wealth of experience in the medical field for a period of 10 years.It was nurtured by the other directors with rich experience in their related fields. This eventually bore fruits by actualizing it into a limited company and eventually as a pharmaceutical company.

The company is poised to sore high and spread its wings in the whole country by making its brands available in all its target market through the vast relations of its directors. In this regards the support from the suppliers will be of great importance in providing quality products and timely supply to avoid out of stock.

The company range of products will cater for different pharmaceutical markets.

These products consist of:

  • Machines and equipment
  • Hospital consumables
  • Ophthalmology products, equipment and consumables
  • Digestive enzymes
  • Cough syrups (both herbal and allopathic)
  • Antihistamines
  • Antimalarial drugs
  • Non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs
  • Skincare products
  • Nutraceuticals etc.
