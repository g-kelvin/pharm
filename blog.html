SEEKING MEDICAL CARE

See a doctor immediately if you:

Are choking and can't speak
Have difficulty breathing
Find it hard to swallow
Notice blood in your phlegm
Have persistent night sweats, fever and weight loss
Make an appointment to see a doctor if you:

Develop a fever of 38°C or higher
Cough up phlegm that's yellow or green
